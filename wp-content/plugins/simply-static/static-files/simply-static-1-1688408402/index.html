How Did We Start ?

Our History

Leaving something unsolved or unattended allows it grow till it demands our urgent time and attention. It was in such a situation, quite a large number of senior old students of KKTM College designed KKTMSEEDS and started the WhatsApp groups.The system would fulfill our aspirations for commitment to the Society and surprisingly this experiment was widely accepted as if the old students were impatiently waiting for such a system and the motivation gained by it , the KKTMSEEDS was registered  as a Charitable Society under the Travancore-Cochin Literary Scientific and Charitable Societies Act XII of 1955 of Govt of Kerala on 16/7/2020 with Registration No. TSR/TC/214/2020.
